AI Intern, Fidelity Investments

Bangalore, India (Aug 2020 - Present)

Fidelity Investments' retirement-plan record keeping is worth over $2 trillion. The company processes tens of thousands of 401(k) Agreements each year. I am working on Document Layout Analysis and Automatic Information Extraction from these 401(k) Adoption Agreements. My work entails using Computer Vision to section lengthy, semi-structured documents and pick up information from the text in these forms using language models. Given the large number form elements and the varying PDF templates from different vendors, this is a complex process. This digitization is set to revolutionaize the company's record-keeping business.

Tech used: Python, OpenCV, Tensorflow, NLTK


AIOps Intern, Gap Inc.

Hyderabad, India (May 2019 - July 2019)

Gap Inc. is a global omni-channel apparel retailer with over 3600 stores and 135,000 employees worldwide. Many IT employees are involved in resolving issues across Gap's network of stores. As part of the monitoring team, I built an AIOps tool which could aid managers to keep a tab on employee and team performances as well as Key Performance Indicators. I built dashboards with visualizations of major performance metrics. These visualizations were customizable by time, employees, teams and other constraints. I also worked on topic modelling for clustering issue descriptions and performed time series analysis to predict the volume of issues for the next 2 years.

Tech used: Python, REST APIs, Splunk, ServiceNow, Jira, NLTK

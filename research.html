Research Projects

Automated Diagnosis of Ductal Carcinoma (Breast Cancer) from FNAC Images with Deep Learning

This is a Computer-Aided Diagnosis project aimed at speeding up diagnosis and easing the Breast Cancer burden on diagnostic labs. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) were used to detect microscopic cell clusters in whole slide images from Fine Needle Aspiration and classify them as benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). I labeled, annotated and augmented cytology images collected from two Pathology Labs. I then performed a comparative study on the efficiency of CNN classifiers (AlexNet, VGG16, 19, ResNet-50, Inception-V3) for cytology images and built a custom CNN classifier which achieved the highest accuracy (97%). To minimize the time and effort needed for data preparation, I integrated YOLO for automatic Region Proposal and later used YOLO as a fully-independent pipeline for cell cluster detection and classification.

COVID-19 Diagnosis and Affected Lung Lesion Segmentation in CT-scans

CT scanning has become a preferred screening process at hospitals to screen patients not showing conventional symptoms of COVID-19. Automating the diagnosis of COVID from CT scans can prove helpful in handling high case-loads. I have assembled CT scans from healthy patients and patients having COVID-19, Tuberculosis, Pneumonia and Flu and am using CNNs for multiclass classification by extracting spaatial features from volumetric slices. I am also formulating solutions to detect ground-glass nodules in lungs.

PII (Personally Identifiable Information) Identification and Redaction in Documents

PII and its usage have been under major scrutiny and have given rise to many laws like GDPR and CCPA in recent years. Many companies struggle to comply with these laws due to the costs and time involved in removing PII before processing or using data. Therefore, it makes sense to build capabilities that automatically identify and redact PII. I have attempted Named Entity Recognition with a SpaCy model for data scaped from various sources. I am testing the efficacy of Bi-LSTMs and Transformers for NER.
